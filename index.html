About A&O Apparels

A&O Apparels (Pvt.) Ltd. is a 100% export oriented woven garments manufacturing company located at 256, Char Rajapur, Shashongaon, Baktobali, Fatullah, Narayanganj, Bangladesh.

Established in 2019, the company focuses on quality garments production, washing & dyeing facility, compliance, modern machinery and reliable export service for international markets.

With skilled manpower, experienced management and continuous development, A&O Apparels is committed to delivering quality products, timely shipment and long-term customer satisfaction.
